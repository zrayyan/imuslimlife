Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the prayer times?

Salate Mustaqeem uses precise calculation methods based on your exact GPS location. You can also choose from multiple calculation methods (MWL, ISNA, Egypt, Makkah, Karachi, Tehran, Jafari) to match your local mosque's schedule.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, once you've loaded your prayer times and Quran data, most features work offline. The app stores your data locally so you can access prayer times, Qibla direction, and read the Quran without an internet connection.

How do I get the widget on my iPhone?

After installing the app, long-press on your home screen and tap the "+" button in the top left corner. Search for "Salate Mustaqeem" and choose the widget size you prefer. You can add multiple widgets for different prayer information.

Is there a premium version of the app?

Salate Mustaqeem provides all essential features for free. We believe that prayer tools should be accessible to everyone. There is an optional donation feature that unlocks additional themes and removes ads to support ongoing development.
